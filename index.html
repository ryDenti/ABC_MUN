About Model United Nations

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation where students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. Participants role-play as diplomats representing countries or NGOs in simulated UN committees.

At ABC MUN, we aim to provide an immersive experience that fosters critical thinking, public speaking, negotiation, and leadership skills.
